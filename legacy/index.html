About ringwormGO

ringwormGO is closed as of May 23, 2025!

We are a non-profit software development group.

Most of our work is focused on low level projects and desktop applications.

For any issues/bugs and suggestions, contact us at project's repository.

FAQ

Q: How did ringwormGO start?

A: We started with web browsers made in VB.NET and eventually evolved into operating systems, low level and desktop applications.


Q: Who are founders of this project?

A: Andrej Bartulin and Stjepan Bilić Matišić (retired)


Q: Are ringwormGO projects free?

A: They are FOSS, free and open source. Some projects can be licensed under different terms if worked in partnership with third party.
